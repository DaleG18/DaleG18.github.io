About Us

Diabolical Gorillas LLC. is a non-profit organization dedicated to saving the gorillas. We work day in and day out in the hopes of saving gorillas from the brink of extinction. The name diabolical gorillas derives from the misconception of gorillas and their ill-nature. We decided to name ourselves this because contrary to the name we too are sweet and elegant as the gorilla truly is.

  • We are non-profit
  • We are black owned
  • We support all races,genders,etc
  • We are motivated!
  • We are kind!
