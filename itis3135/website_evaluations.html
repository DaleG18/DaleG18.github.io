Arngren Electronics

Argren Electronics is a Norweigian Electronics company that was founded in 1983. They mostly sell gadgets such as bikes, dune buggies, scooters etc. They are widely known for how terrible there website is, being ranked as one of the worst websites of 2020 on multiples pages.

  • Constrast
  • Repitition
  • Alignment
  • Proximity

The site makes use of contrast by using a white background with bright and easy to read font colors, but fails majorly in repitition, alignment, and proximity. There seems to be no repitition, as every image is randomly scattered, making the alignment and proximity also fail to be evident with no real structure to the page.

Rolling Stone Magazine

Rolling Stone is a world renowned magazine company that has been established since 1967. They currently have over 700,000 copies issued across America monthly. They came to fame by becoming one of the first magazines to cover rock music and have become a household name for the critique and interview of music and musicians alike.

  • Contrast
  • Repitition
  • Alignment
  • Proximity

The Rolling Stone website makes use of contrast, alignment and repition greatly by using bright vibrant colors against a white background and allowing a similar style throughout the website with the use of whitespace and grids. The website also makes use of proximity by using headers to keep relevant information close together.
